Solenoid Valve

About Solenoid Valves

Solenoid valves are electromechanically operated valves used to control the flow of liquids or gases.

Features:

  • Quick response time
  • Reliable and compact design
  • Low power consumption

Applications:

Used in pneumatic systems, fluid power systems, water treatment plants, and industrial automation.
